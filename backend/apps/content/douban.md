# Douban (豆瓣)

Your watched movies, read books, listened music, and reviews — arguably the best long-form personal media log from the Chinese internet. No export button, so we scrape.

## What you can export

- Watched / wish-to-watch movies with ratings and reviews
- Read / wish-to-read books with ratings and reviews
- Listened / wish-to-listen music
- Friends (broadcasters) and groups

## Recommended

1. No official export.
2. Agent logs in with your cookies and walks book/movie/music.douban.com/people/<your-id>/.
3. Paginates each tab, extracts JSON.
4. Rate-limits politely.
