# Spotify

Your complete listening history — every song, every skip, back to account creation if you request 'Extended streaming history'.

## What you can export

- Streaming history (short: 1 year) or **Extended** (all time)
- Playlists (owned + followed)
- Library (saved tracks, albums, artists)
- Podcast listening + followed shows
- Account + inferred data

## Recommended

1. **spotify.com/account/privacy** → scroll to **Download your data**.
2. Check **Account data** + **Extended streaming history** (for full archive).
3. Confirm via email; wait **up to 30 days** for Extended (regular comes in a few days).
4. Unpack the zip into `imports/spotify/`.
