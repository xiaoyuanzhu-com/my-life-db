# Garmin Connect

Everything Garmin knows about you — routes, biometrics, and every breath during a workout.

## What you can export

- Activities as FIT/TCX/GPX
- Daily wellness (steps, HR, stress, Body Battery)
- Sleep sessions and stages
- Personal records, segments, courses
- Device + profile metadata

## Recommended

1. Go to **garmin.com/account/datamanagement/exportdata** → **Request Data**.
2. Wait for the email (can take hours to days).
3. Download the zip — includes one folder per data type.
4. Drop the zip in; keep original structure.
