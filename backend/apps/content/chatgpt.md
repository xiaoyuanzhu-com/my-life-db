# ChatGPT

Your full conversation history with ChatGPT, exportable as JSON.

## What you can export

- All conversations (JSON + pretty HTML)
- Custom GPTs and memories
- Feedback history
- Account metadata

## Recommended

1. **chat.openai.com** → profile → **Settings** → **Data Controls**.
2. Click **Export Data** → confirm via email.
3. Wait for the email (minutes-to-hours).
4. Download the zip.
