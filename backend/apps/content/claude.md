# Claude

Your full conversation history with Claude, exportable as JSON.

## What you can export

- All conversations (JSON)
- Projects and project context
- Account and workspace metadata

## Recommended

1. **claude.ai** → profile → **Settings** → **Privacy**.
2. Click **Export Data** → confirm via email.
3. Wait for the email.
4. Download the zip.
