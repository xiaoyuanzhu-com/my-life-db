# Instagram

Your posts, stories, messages, followers, and every piece of metadata Meta stores about your Instagram presence.

## What you can export

- Posts, stories, reels (media + captions)
- DMs and voice messages
- Likes, saves, comments
- Followers and following
- Profile + activity metadata

## Recommended

1. Go to **accountscenter.instagram.com/info_and_permissions/dyi/**.
2. Choose **JSON** (easier to parse than HTML).
3. Select date range + content types.
4. Wait for the email (hours to a day).
5. Download the zip; keep the structure.
