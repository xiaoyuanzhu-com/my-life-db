# Strava

Your GPS activities, kudos, and goal history from the athletic social network.

## What you can export

- Activities (GPX, FIT, TCX)
- Activity index CSV (with titles, times, gear)
- Kudos and comments
- Routes, segments, gear
- Profile + follower/following lists

## Recommended

1. Log in → go to **strava.com/athlete/delete_your_account** (don't worry — that's Strava's export page too).
2. Click **Get Started** under **Download Request**.
3. Wait for the email (usually hours).
4. Download the zip; keep the structure.
